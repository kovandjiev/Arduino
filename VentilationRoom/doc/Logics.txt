Base topic. Use it for broadcast. See 1 Callback
Device name. Example: room_name.

On and close off ventilation. For them switch relay 1 for open and 2 for close. The duration is 40 seconds.

After the device has started first operation is to be off the ventilate.

topic:payload

Callback:
1 base_topic:null - broadcast command, respond with base_topic/device_name:OK
2 base_topic/device_name:null - respond with base_topic/device_name:OK
3 base_topic/device_name:all - send all data per deveice
4 base_topic/device_name/ventilate/set:[on | off] - open/close the ventilation
5 base_topic/device_name/windows:null - respond with windows state

Publish:
 base_topic/device_name:ready - The device has jet stated. This message need to send initialize settings from the remote server. It should publish: data from the server.
 base_topic/device_name/ventilate:[on | off] - open or close a ventilation
 base_topic/device_name/windows:[open | close] - open or close all windows
 base_topic/device_name/window/1:[open | close] - open or close a window 1
 base_topic/device_name/window/2:[open | close] - open or close a window 2
 base_topic/device_name/window/3:[open | close] - open or close a window 3
 base_topic/device_name/window/4:[open | close] - open or close a window 4
